Preparation:

  • wash lantiles at leste 3 times untile water runes clean
  • cut onion in small cubes
  • cut tomato in small cubes
  • cut green chillis in small pices
  • make past of ginger and garlic
  • choped 2 to 3 garlic for tadka

Instructions:

For Dal

  1. in a cooker add oil or ghee
  2. add onions and chillis and fry onion for 2 minetes
  3. add all the spices
  4. add water
  5. let the dal boil

For Tadka

  1. in a smoll pan add ghee or oil
  2. add kadhipatta
  3. add musterd seeds
  4. add cumin
  5. add 1 or 2 dry red chilli
  6. add choped garlic
  7. fry every thing until garlic becomes broun and soft and musterd seeds starts poping
  8. turn off gas and add red chilli powder to tadka
  9. add the tadka in the dal and close the lead for 1 minetes
  10. let the dal boil for 5 to 10 minetes
  11. add water acording to the cunsistency of the dal
  12. garnish with coriender
  13. eat with rice or roti (Whole wheet bread)
